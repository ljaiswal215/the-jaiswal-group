ABOUT SENDERO COLLECTION

MODERN ARCHITECTURE. ELEVATED LIVING.

Built by Tri Pointe Homes between 2021 and 2022, Sendero Collection stands as one of the newest and most architecturally refined luxury neighborhoods in Pacific Highlands Ranch. This intimate collection of 81 detached single-family homes brings a fresh, contemporary vision to the northern portion of PHR — one that pairs elevated design with the community’s signature lifestyle amenities.

The neighborhood’s residential streets — Fischer Way, Cayucos Lane, and Paloma Meadows — reflect the thoughtful planning behind Sendero Collection. Every detail, from the street layout to the individual homesite selections, was considered with an eye toward privacy, curb appeal, and the Southern California indoor-outdoor lifestyle.

Sendero Collection homes are available in three distinct elevation styles: Modern Farmhouse, Traditional Spanish, and Modern — each featuring clean lines, expansive windows, and welcoming outdoor living spaces. The variety of architectural expressions gives the neighborhood a dynamic yet cohesive streetscape that distinguishes it from earlier-generation communities in PHR.

Floor plans range from approximately 3,010 to 3,455 square feet and offer four to six bedrooms alongside flexible lofts, bonus spaces, and optional guest suites. Open-concept layouts, oversized kitchen islands, walk-in pantries, and covered outdoor living areas create homes designed for both everyday comfort and entertaining at scale.

Sendero Collection was designed with the future in mind. Homes feature energy-efficient construction, solar readiness, smart-home technology integration, and EV charging infrastructure — features that align with the preferences of today’s discerning buyers and add lasting value over time.

Residents are minutes from The Village at Pacific Highlands Ranch for everyday shopping, dining, and services, and enjoy access to the community’s over 1,300 acres of preserved open space and an extensive network of scenic trails.

Families are drawn to Sendero Collection for its access to highly regarded public schools. Students are generally served by the Solana Beach School District and San Dieguito Union High School District, with attendance at Solana Santa Fe Elementary, Pacific Trails Middle School, and either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School. School attendance boundaries should always be confirmed directly with the applicable district.

Beyond the neighborhood itself, Sendero Collection’s northern PHR location provides easy access to the beaches of Del Mar, One Paseo, Del Mar Highlands Town Center, and convenient freeway access via Interstate 5 and State Route 56.

Sendero Collection is governed by the West Highlands Pacific HOA.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

ABOUT SENDERO COLLECTION

What schools serve Sendero Collection?

Students residing in Sendero Collection are generally assigned to Solana Santa Fe Elementary School, Pacific Trails Middle School, and either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School. The community is served by the Solana Beach School District and San Dieguito Union High School District. As attendance boundaries and enrollment policies may change, we recommend confirming school assignments directly with the applicable school district.

Is Sendero Collection part of an HOA?

Yes. Sendero Collection is governed by the West Highlands Pacific HOA. Residents enjoy access to community pools, fitness facilities, gathering spaces, and the 1,300+ acres of preserved open space and trails throughout PHR. Contact us for current HOA fee details.
