ABOUT RANCHO GLENS ESTATES

ESTATE LIVING. A RARE FIND IN CARMEL VALLEY.

Rancho Glens Estates is one of Carmel Valley’s most exclusive and rarely available communities. Originally approved in the late 1980s and developed through the 1990s, this private gated enclave predates the larger Pacific Highlands Ranch master plan development and represents a truly distinctive residential experience in 92130.

With just 36 estate residences accessed through a private gated entrance off Caminito Mendiola, Rancho Glens Estates offers a level of privacy, space, and architectural character that is exceptionally difficult to find anywhere in Carmel Valley or the surrounding areas. Custom and semi-custom homes throughout the community reflect a range of architectural styles — including French Country, Tuscan, Mediterranean, and Spanish — each one a unique expression of the homeowner’s vision and craftsmanship.

One of the most notable characteristics of Rancho Glens Estates is its abundance of single-story homes — a rarity in 92130 and a feature that draws significant interest from buyers seeking the convenience and accessibility of one-level living at a luxury scale. Homes range from approximately 3,800 to 7,400 or more square feet and offer two to six bedrooms, with homesites that can extend to 1.5 acres or more.

These expansive parcels provide room for swimming pools, guest houses, sports courts, vegetable gardens, and mature landscape installations that are simply not achievable on standard-sized 92130 lots. Long driveways, established trees, and thoughtfully designed outdoor spaces give each residence a sense of arrival and privacy that is unmatched in the broader neighborhood.

Rancho Glens Estates benefits from a prime location near the Village at Pacific Highlands Ranch, PHR Community Park, One Paseo, Del Mar Highlands Town Center, and the beaches of Del Mar. Quick access to Interstate 5 and State Route 56 places the broader San Diego metro and North County coastal destinations within easy reach.

From a financial standpoint, Rancho Glens Estates carries a significant advantage: the community does not carry Mello-Roos special tax assessments. This is a major distinction compared to nearly all newer Pacific Highlands Ranch neighborhoods, and it translates to meaningful long-term savings for buyers who factor total carrying costs into their purchase decisions.

School assignments for residents of Rancho Glens Estates may vary by parcel. Elementary school options in the area include Pacific Sky Elementary. Middle school students generally attend Pacific Trails Middle School (7–8), and high school students are served by either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School (9–12) through the San Dieguito Union High School District. School enrollment and attendance boundaries should always be verified directly with the Del Mar Union School District and San Dieguito Union High School District.

For buyers seeking a custom or semi-custom estate home with privacy, space, architectural distinction, and no Mello-Roos — in one of San Diego’s most sought-after zip codes — Rancho Glens Estates stands in a class of its own.

ABOUT RANCHO GLENS ESTATES

What schools serve Rancho Glens Estates?

School assignments for Rancho Glens Estates may vary by parcel. Elementary school options in the area include Pacific Sky Elementary. Middle school students generally attend Pacific Trails Middle School (7–8), and high school students are served by either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School (9–12) through the San Dieguito Union High School District. We strongly recommend confirming assignments directly with the Del Mar Union School District and San Dieguito Union High School District.

Is Rancho Glens Estates part of an HOA?

Yes. Rancho Glens Estates is a private gated community with its own HOA. Importantly, the community does not carry Mello-Roos assessments — a significant financial advantage compared to newer PHR neighborhoods. HOA dues and governing documents are available upon request.
