ABOUT VISTA SANTA FE

EXPANSIVE HOMESITES. ELEVATED LIVING.

Completed by Pardee Homes in 2020, Vista Santa Fe is one of the most exclusive collections of luxury residences ever built within Pacific Highlands Ranch. With only 44 homes, this rare enclave occupies an elevated position in PHR North — offering canyon vistas and sweeping views across the rolling landscapes of Rancho Santa Fe that few communities in the region can match.

The community’s Desert Modern aesthetic draws on the clean lines, expansive glass, and seamless indoor-outdoor living that define contemporary Southern California architecture, while the elevated topography and generous site selection ensure that homes feel both grand and private. Streets including Aurora Summit Trails, Morning Light Trails, and Skyglitter Trails wind through a setting that is intimate by design — a deliberate choice that preserves the neighborhood’s sense of exclusivity.

Homes at Vista Santa Fe range from approximately 4,356 to 4,922 square feet across four to five bedrooms, with both single-story and two-story floor plans available — a rarity within Pacific Highlands Ranch that broadens the community’s appeal to buyers seeking the convenience of single-level living at a luxury scale. Homesites approaching three-quarters of an acre provide the space needed for expansive outdoor rooms, resort-style pools, outdoor kitchens, and guest accommodations, making Vista Santa Fe one of the most customizable luxury communities in North County Coastal San Diego.

Residents enjoy an exceptional amenity advantage: access to both the West Highland Pacific Recreation Center and The Club at Lopelia Meadows. Together, these two recreation centers provide a combined offering of resort-style pools, spas, fitness facilities, and gathering spaces that rivals what many master-planned communities offer exclusively to their entire resident base.

The Vista Santa Fe Trail connects residents directly to PHR’s broader trail network and more than 1,300 acres of preserved open space — providing abundant opportunities for hiking, cycling, and outdoor recreation steps from the front door.

Families are drawn to Vista Santa Fe in part for its access to highly regarded schools. Students are generally assigned to Solana Santa Fe Elementary School within the Solana Beach School District, followed by Pacific Trails Middle School, and either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School within the San Dieguito Union High School District. Enrollment and attendance boundaries should always be verified directly with the applicable district.

The Village at Pacific Highlands Ranch, Del Mar beaches, One Paseo, Del Mar Highlands Town Center, and convenient connectivity to Interstate 5 and State Route 56 ensure that residents enjoy the full range of North County Coastal amenities without sacrificing the privacy and seclusion that make Vista Santa Fe so distinctive.

Vista Santa Fe is governed by the Pacific Highlands Ranch HOA and is subject to Mello-Roos special tax assessments.

With only 44 homes ever built, Vista Santa Fe is among the most rarely available luxury addresses in all of Pacific Highlands Ranch — representing an exceptional opportunity for buyers seeking expansive homesites, elevated views, and resort-caliber living in one of San Diego’s most desirable master-planned communities.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

ABOUT VISTA SANTA FE

What schools serve Vista Santa Fe?

Students residing in Vista Santa Fe are generally assigned to Solana Santa Fe Elementary School within the Solana Beach School District, then Pacific Trails Middle School, and either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School within the San Dieguito Union High School District. As attendance boundaries and enrollment policies may change, we recommend confirming school assignments directly with the applicable school district.

Is Vista Santa Fe part of an HOA?

Yes. Vista Santa Fe is governed by the Pacific Highlands Ranch HOA. Residents enjoy access to both the West Highland Pacific Recreation Center and The Club at Lopelia Meadows, offering resort-style pools, spas, fitness facilities, and gathering spaces. The community is also subject to Mello-Roos special tax assessments. Contact us for current HOA fees and Mello-Roos details.
