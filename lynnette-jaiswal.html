Her Story

DRIVEN BY A PERSONAL MISSION

Lynnette Jaiswal is a native San Diegan who has spent her entire life in the community she now serves. Before real estate, she spent more than five years in hospital administration and medical education — a career that taught her to operate with precision, care, and the highest standards of service under pressure.

Her path into real estate came from a deeply personal place. After a frustrating experience with poor agent representation during her own home purchase, Lynnette decided to be the change she wished had existed. She entered the industry with a single purpose: to be the kind of agent her clients could truly trust — one who puts their interests first, communicates with complete honesty, and never leaves them guessing.

That commitment has defined her career. Whether she is guiding a first-time buyer, helping a family relocate from across the country, or marketing a luxury property, Lynnette brings the same unwavering standard of care to every transaction.

The Jaiswal Group

A BOUTIQUE BROKERAGE BUILT WITHOUT RED TAPE

In 2024, Lynnette became a licensed California Real Estate Broker and founded The Jaiswal Group, Inc. — her own independent boutique brokerage. The decision was deliberate: she wanted the freedom to serve clients the way they deserved to be served, without the bureaucratic constraints that larger firms impose.

As an independent broker, Lynnette can move faster, negotiate harder, and craft truly customized strategies for every client. There is no corporate playbook — just a direct relationship, expert guidance, and a team small enough to care deeply about every transaction.
