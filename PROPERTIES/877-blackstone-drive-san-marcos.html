About This Home

LIVE. WORK. RELAX.

Welcome home to this modern sanctuary nestled within the gated community of Rancho Tesoro in North City San Marcos. Thoughtfully designed and built in 2021, this residence embodies the essence of contemporary California living — where comfort, efficiency, and flexibility come together seamlessly.

This 2-bedroom, 2-bath, 1,251 sq. ft. home offers a highly functional layout enhanced by a versatile bonus space on the entry level — ideal for a home office, gym, media room, or additional living area. The main living level features an open-concept design connecting the kitchen, dining, and living areas.

The chef-inspired kitchen includes quartz countertops, upgraded cabinetry, stainless steel appliances, and a peninsula with bar seating. The primary suite provides a comfortable retreat, complete with a walk-in closet and a well-appointed ensuite bath featuring dual vanities and a spacious walk-in shower. A secondary bedroom and full bathroom offer additional flexibility for guests or extended use.

Elevated features throughout the home include luxury vinyl plank flooring, upgraded carpeting, recessed lighting, tankless water heater, fully paid solar, and an attached 2-car garage equipped with EV charging capability — delivering both comfort and long-term energy efficiency.
